Simulation Engine
Simulation

Model complexity.
Remove uncertainty.

Traditional mine planning relies on spreadsheet averages and rules of thumb. SimulatOre replaces guesswork with high-fidelity agent-based and discrete event simulation, capturing the stochastic nature of real mining operations — equipment variability, weather, shift patterns, and downstream bottlenecks.

  • Agent calendars with shift-level granularity and efficiency profiles
  • Commodity type management for ore, waste, and consumables
  • Activity sequencing with predecessor and dependency logic
  • Results visualization and downloadable output datasets
  • Simulation status tracking and job queue monitoring
Optimization Engine
Optimization

Find the optimal.
Not just the feasible.

SimulatOre integrates mathematical optimization solvers directly into simulation workflows via webhooks and API. Define your objective function — maximize throughput, minimize cost, balance fleet utilization — and let the solver find provably optimal solutions constrained by your operational realities.

  • Mixed-integer linear programming for scheduling and allocation
  • Optimization workflow integration with webhook triggers
  • Constraint management for equipment, resource, and time limits
  • Sensitivity analysis and trade-off visualization
  • Bulk scenario generation and comparison

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about SimulatOre — the simulation, optimization, and predictive modelling platform from Epiroc.

What is SimulatOre?

SimulatOre is Epiroc's integrated product suite for mine simulation, production optimization, and predictive modelling. It allows mining teams to build digital representations of their operations, run simulations, identify bottlenecks, optimize schedules, and forecast outcomes — all in a web-based collaborative platform.

What types of simulation does SimulatOre support?

SimulatOre supports agent-based simulation, discrete event simulation, network flow modelling, and activity-based sequencing. It handles underground and open-pit mining scenarios including haulage, blasting, drilling, loading, and material transport across the entire mining value chain.

How does SimulatOre support production optimization?

SimulatOre integrates mathematical optimization solvers — including linear programming and mixed-integer programming — with simulation workflows. Users define objectives such as maximizing throughput or minimizing cost, apply operational constraints, and the platform finds provably optimal solutions for production schedules and resource allocations.

What predictive modelling capabilities are available?

SimulatOre enables predictive modelling through machine learning and statistical methods applied to operational data. Teams can forecast equipment health and failures, production rates, ore quality, and cycle times — enabling proactive decision-making that reduces unplanned downtime and improves operational reliability.

Do I need to install any software?

No. SimulatOre is a fully web-based platform accessible from any modern browser. There is no software to install, no licenses to manage on individual machines, and no IT infrastructure to maintain. Teams collaborate in real-time from any location.

Who uses SimulatOre?

SimulatOre is used by mining engineers, production planners, technical service teams, and operational analysts at mining companies worldwide. It supports both single-site and multi-site operations across commodities including gold, copper, iron ore, lithium, and coal.

How does SimulatOre integrate with existing systems?

SimulatOre provides REST API endpoints and webhook-based optimization workflow integration, enabling connection to fleet management systems, mine planning tools, and enterprise data platforms. Data can also be uploaded directly via structured datasets.

Is SimulatOre part of the Epiroc product portfolio?

Yes. SimulatOre is developed and maintained by Epiroc, a leading productivity partner for the mining and infrastructure industries. It is part of Epiroc's Digital Solutions portfolio, complementing its world-class drilling, loading, and rock excavation equipment with analytical intelligence.
